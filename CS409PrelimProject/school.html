Mission

Crownwell University is committed to:

  • Providing transformative, holistic, and world-class education that empowers students to excel in their fields and contribute meaningfully to society.
  • Advancing cutting-edge research and innovation that address local and global challenges.
  • Nurturing a culture of critical thinking, integrity, and inclusivity, grounded in service to the nation and the global community.
  • Building strong partnerships with industries, communities, and institutions to foster sustainable growth and social impact.
  • Forming ethical, competent, and visionary leaders who embody excellence, compassion, and a commitment to lifelong learning.

    • Vision

    Crownwell University envisions itself as a premier global center of excellence in education, research, and innovation, cultivating leaders and professionals who drive social transformation, sustainable development, and nation-building in the Philippines and beyond.

    About Crownwell University

    Crownwell University stands as a beacon of academic excellence, innovation, and leadership in the Philippines and the world. Founded on the ideals of knowledge, service, and integrity, the University has built a reputation as a premier institution where tradition meets innovation and where future leaders are shaped.

    With a diverse community of scholars, thought leaders, and professionals, Prestige University offers world-class education across disciplines, empowering students to thrive in a rapidly changing global landscape. Guided by its strong commitment to research and social responsibility, the University actively contributes solutions to local and international challenges while fostering values of inclusivity, ethical leadership, and lifelong learning.

    At Prestige University, students are not only prepared for successful careers but are also inspired to make meaningful impact in society. More than an academic institution, it is a place where passion meets purpose, and where excellence is pursued with vision and heart.

    Our History

    Crownwell University was founded in 2010 with a vision to create a modern institution that combines academic excellence, innovation, and service to society. What began as a small college offering limited programs has grown into a premier university recognized for its strong research initiatives, industry partnerships, and global outlook. In just over a decade, Crownwell University has established itself as one of the fastest-rising higher education institutions in the Philippines, shaping leaders ready for the challenges of the 21st century.

    • 2013: Gained university status.

    Campus Facilities

    Prestige University boasts state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance the learning experience and support student development.

    University Library

    Our five-story library houses over 500,000 volumes, 200+ research databases, and numerous study spaces including private study rooms and collaborative workspaces.

    Research Labs

    Cutting-edge research facilities equipped with the latest technology, supporting innovation in sciences, engineering, and health disciplines.

    Sports Complex

    A 100,000 sq ft facility featuring an Olympic-sized pool, basketball courts, fitness center, rock climbing wall, and indoor track.

    Dining Halls

    Multiple dining options across campus offering diverse cuisines, dietary accommodations, and sustainable food practices.

    Technology Center

    24/7 access to computer labs, maker spaces, 3D printers, and multimedia production studios.

    Outdoor Spaces

    Beautifully landscaped gardens, recreational fields, and outdoor study areas across our 200-acre campus.
